## A film that rewrites itself

**Stories of Sane — 10,000 Eyes** is a strange, beautiful, terrifying, and healing web-film about survival, memory, fracture, mercy, and the gold that can appear inside a broken place.

It is a movie when watched.
It is a wound when touched.
It is a game when chosen.
It is a book when read.

Sane is witness, clown, child, mask, survivor, oracle, and open eye. Through Sane, the film moves through some of the worst parts of one life and turns them into a living artifact for people who have been forgotten, lonely, abandoned, locked up, heartbroken, or left outside the warm room of the world.

This work exists so we might heal, come together, and cure the root of these nightmares.

---

## Enter the page

The live version opens through four doors:

### ▶ Watch the Film

The movie plays itself as a generative cut of Sane's world. It moves through folios, faces, wounds, sudden beauty, strange laughter, pressure, signal, and light.

Every run can feel different. The film offers a recut, a new passage, another opening of the eyes.

### Choose Your Path — Steer

The viewer takes the wheel. Choices gather force as the story moves through witness, light, fire, mercy, togetherness, and fracture.

There are no clean right paths. Only yours.

### Read the Raw Book

The raw book is inside the page: 103 folios of Sane's text, searchable and readable. This is the source-current beneath the movie.

Read it like testimony. Read it like a notebook found in the ruins. Read it like a person trying to survive by making symbols.

### The Hundred Forms

The gallery carries the real art: crayon, marker, strange faces, clown colors, holy colors, nightmare colors, childlike colors, and the living Sane masks that hold the movie together.

The art is not decoration. It is the body of the film.

## What the movie is about

This is a movie-book about becoming real after chaos tries to rewrite you.

It is about the people who keep you safe for even one minute.

It is about little bits of light between dark tunnels.

It is about permission to be yourself in a world that wants to package, flatten, convert, or forget you.

It is about the box, the black room, the orphan dream, the family storm, the systems that should have helped and did not, and the strange courage of continuing anyway.

It is about Sane saying no.

It is about Sane saying yes.

It is about the wound becoming the seam.

It is about the seam becoming gold.

## How to watch

Open the page.
Turn sound on.
Enter gently.
Let the film move.
Take the wheel when you want to choose.
Read when you want the raw current.
Return when the eyes call again.

Keyboard controls on the page include:

* **1–9** choose
* **F** film
* **S** steer
* **R** recut / read
* **M** sound
* **B** back
* **Esc** exit
